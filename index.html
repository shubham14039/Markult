The Marketing society

Since its inception, Markult has evolved from a small collegiate society into a dynamic platform that nurtures a vibrant community. Originally founded with the goal of enhancing student employability and skill development, Markult has grown into a robust association by and for students. Our activities range from hosting events designed to prepare students for management roles to tackling real-world challenges faced by various organizations. Our mission is to equip students to be corporate-ready while creating cherished memories that enrich their college experience. Markult's influence extends beyond the confines of Motilal Nehru College, proudly representing our institution and achieving recognition far and wide. With over 4,400 students engaged, hosting 7,000 participants, and collaborating with more than 100 societies, Markult stands as a beacon of inspiration and achievement. Our journey has been a thrilling roller coaster, with all the twists and turns of a blockbuster movie. At Markult, we're not just preparing for the future; we're making history.

Our Vision:

Our vision is to create a distinguished platform that not only recognizes the strategic and innovative contributions of our vibrant community of student leaders and advisors but also acts as a catalyst for marketing excellence and comprehensive development.

Our Mission:

Markult is dedicated to weaving a rich tapestry of stories, insights, and scholarly discourse that:

  • Celebrates the unique journeys and achievements of our members.
  • Engages with complex marketing challenges and presents in-depth case studies to spark intellectual curiosity and enhance practical problem-solving skills.
  • Fosters a community of learners and practitioners committed to the advancement of marketing knowledge and the upholdment of ethical practices.

    • Our events:

    SYMPOSIUM'23: Premier Flagship Event

    Symposium’23 set a new benchmark as a powerhouse event, drawing an unprecedented 8,000+ attendees and 60,000+ online impressions. With 3,000+ applications from prestigious institutions such as IITs, IIMs, and MDIs, the event was a melting pot of ideas and talent, leaving a lasting impact on all who attended.

    COMIENZO 2.0: April 2023 Sophomore Semi-Annual Event

    Building on the success of our first Comienzo, Comienzo 2.0 took place on April 3, 2023. This sequel event captivated 5,000+ attendees and generated 50,000 impressions, cementing its status as a must-attend for those passionate about marketing. The competitive yet enjoyable atmosphere was a hit among students, sparking creativity and strategic thinking.

    COMIENZO: Inaugural Event November 2022

    Markult’s inaugural event, Comienzo, launched on November 24, 2022, was a resounding success. Attracting over 2,000 applications and 30,000 online impressions, the competition featured participants from over 30 top colleges, including IITs and IIMs. Winners walked away with prizes totaling over ₹1,00,000, setting a high bar for future events.
